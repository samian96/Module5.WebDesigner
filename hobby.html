One of my main hobbies is working on my motorcycles as well as going for weekend rides.

This is my daily commuter, a 2007 Suzuki boulevard S40. It is a 650cc single cylinder cruiser style bike, might not be the fastest bike but it gets the job done. Very beginner friendly, as well as reliable and easy to maintain mechanically.

This is my weekend bike/project bike.

It is a 2004 Kawasaki Ninja 500r. Most of the time this bike is spent in the garage trying to fix all the problems. Occassionally I do get to take it out and ride for a bit, but with any older bike that has been wrecked problems show up. The carburator has been rebuilt as well as the top end of the engine.
